A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Science Quiz Competition

Instructions

  • This Online Quiz Contest program is aimed to encourage children to check their general knowledge and improve their performance & confidence

  • Quiz Questions are arranged randomly on various topics fit in to General Knowledge, Current affairs, sports, History, Science & Technology,Mental ability, Culture, Heritage etc

  • Participants can enter the quiz any number of times and only latest details will taken into the consideration for the contest

  • Quiz questions will be changed once in every two months

  • Winners will be published on this portal page and will be informed by e-mail (if provided).All the participants are requested to fill their correct details and insufficient details will not be taken ino the consideration.

  • This quiz is opened to classes between 3rd class to 10th class only and is divided into three groups. Classes 3rd to 5th considered as one group,6th to 8th considered as second group and classes 9th and 10th considered as third group. All the three groups will have different set of quiz questions and each class carries different time duration.
